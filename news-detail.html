Amazing Smart Cars to Improve Driving Test Process in Qatar

Share this article

Driving schools are taking a step towards higher standards of transparency by incorporating smart cars into their training programs to improve Driving Test Process in Qatar. These technologically advanced vehicles are capable of assessing driving skills, aiming to alleviate complaints from trainees who fail their driving tests. The Traffic Department, represented by Brigadier Mohammed Saad Al Kharji, Director of the General Directorate of Traffic, has already implemented this initiative in two schools, including the renowned Dallah Driving Academy. Furthermore, plans are underway to introduce these smart cars in other driving schools as well, with the ultimate goal of enhancing services and reducing grievances from trainees check also T-junction Qatar Driving Test .

the introduction of smart cars in driving schools represents a significant step towards transparency, fairness, and technological advancement in the driving test process. By leveraging advanced sensors, cameras, and automated recording systems, these vehicles provide an objective evaluation of driving skills, reducing complaints and instilling confidence in the testing process. The integration of smart cars not only benefits trainees by offering a standardized and unbiased assessment but also contributes to the ongoing improvement of road safety. As the driving industry continues to embrace technological innovations, the incorporation of smart cars paves the way for future advancements in driver training and prepares individuals for the evolving landscape of connected and autonomous vehicles.

The integration of smart cars Improve Driving Test Process in Qatar

The integration of smart cars into the driving test process eliminates the need for direct contact between the applicant and the examiner. Traditionally, the examiner, usually a policeman, had the authority to determine whether a candidate passed or failed the test. However, with the introduction of smart cars, this dynamic will undergo a significant transformation. All driving tests, including parking and other maneuvers, will now be carried out by these intelligent vehicles. The Traffic Department is actively working towards implementing this new scheme across all driving schools, thereby revolutionizing the way driving tests are conducted check also applying driver license Qatar

key advantages of the newly introduced “smart cars”

One of the key advantages of the newly introduced “smart cars” is their ability to independently decide test results, eliminating the need for intervention from supervising policemen. These vehicles are equipped with cameras and have the capability to record the results directly onto the traffic department’s system. This automated process ensures objectivity and impartiality in evaluating a trainee’s performance. Policemen will no longer accompany trainees during the tests; instead, they will remotely monitor the trainees through the cameras installed in the smart cars.
